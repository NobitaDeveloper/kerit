Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Dive into our comprehensive FAQ or reach out for more help.

What is KERiT?

▼

KERiT is a powerful Discord bot offering advanced antinuke protection, automod features, and a wide range of interactive tools for server management and community engagement.

How do I invite KERiT to my server?

▼

Visit our homepage and click "Invite KERiT", or use this direct link: Invite Link.

Is KERiT free to use?

▼

Yes! KERiT is free with core features. Premium unlocks advanced tools like unlimited AI and priority support. Join our server for details.

How do I configure antinuke protection?

▼

Use antinuke enable to start, then customize with antinuke config and specifics like antinuke antiban. Full list on our Commands page.

Can I customize welcome messages?

▼

Absolutely! Commands like greet message add, greet embed, and greet image add let you create stunning welcomes.

How does the ticket system work?

▼

Setup with ticket panel setup, users react to create tickets. Manage with ticket close, ticket rename, and more.

What automod features are available?

▼

Anti-invite, anti-link, anti-spam, caps filter, and bad word detection. Configure via automod config.

Join our support server for real-time assistance from our team and community experts.

Does KERiT support premium features?

▼

Yes, upgrade for enhanced limits and exclusives. Check our pricing page for plans.
